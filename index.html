Configure DNS on Your Router

For whole-network protection, configure DNS filtering at your router level:

  1. Find your router's IP address (usually 192.168.1.1 or 192.168.0.1)
  2. Log in with admin credentials
  3. Navigate to Internet/WAN settings
  4. Replace existing DNS servers with OpenDNS addresses
  5. Save changes and restart your router
