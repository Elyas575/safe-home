Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. SafeHome Blocker is designed for maximum privacy and security.

Our Commitment

SafeHome Blocker does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. All data is stored locally and used only for the purpose of blocking adult content and supporting user recovery.

Details

  • No personal data is ever transmitted to our servers.
  • All filtering and recovery features operate entirely on your device.
  • We do not use tracking cookies or analytics scripts.
  • Your privacy and safety are our top priorities.

Contact

If you have any questions about privacy, please contact our support team.
